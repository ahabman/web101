"Elements" are what create structure. They can be displayed as "block" or "inline".

Block are satacked vertically like all these paragraphs. They take up the width of their containers like a row.

Inline elements like links can appear within block-level or other inline elments. They take up the width of their contents.

Each element implies meaning. The Semantic Web is a good idaea, and it's why we care.
